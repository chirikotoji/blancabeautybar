A note from the founder

Hi, I'm Buthyna.

I opened Blanca Beauty Bar in 2019 with a simple aim: to elevate the nail salon experience. Every detail of the studio, from the chair you sit in to the tools we use, has been considered.

Maryam and I are both trained in the Russian e-file method. We work dry, no soaking, no cutting. Every reusable tool is autoclave-sterilized between clients. Coffee or tea on arrival. The room stays calm on purpose.

Buthyna xo
