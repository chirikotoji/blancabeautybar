How it works

The Russian
method.

Five steps. No water. No cutting. Done properly.

01

You walk in. Coffee or tea?

We greet you, take your jacket, sit you at the chair. The room is small, calm, and on purpose quiet. Espresso, herbal tea , your choice while we set up.

02

Sterile tools come out.

Every reusable tool is autoclave-sterilized between clients , the same standard used on surgical instruments. Single-use abrasives are opened in front of you and disposed of after.

03

The e-file, dry.

No soaking. The cuticle is gently lifted and refined with a professional rotary tool. Never cut. Better for nail health, better for retention.

04

Pick your colour.

250+ gel shades on the wall. Bring an inspo photo or pick something on the day. Gel is applied flush against the skin, sealed at the cuticle line.

05

See you in four to five weeks.

Cured, sealed, oiled. The set is meant to look fresh at week four. Most clients come back at week five for the next.
