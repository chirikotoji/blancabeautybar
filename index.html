Meet Blanca Beauty Bar

Located in the heart of downtown London, Ontario, Blanca Beauty Bar pairs precision treatments with a calm, considered space. Russian e-file manicures, diode laser, and lash & brow services delivered by trained therapists who care about every detail. Walk out feeling cared for.
