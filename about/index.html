A note, from the chair.

Blanca Beauty was founded in 2019 by Buthyna Abu-Deyah. The studio is at 392 Clarence Street in Citi Plaza, downtown London Ontario.

The Founder

Buthyna

Buthyna xo

Blanca Beauty was founded with a singular aim: to elevate the nail salon experience. Every detail has been refined from beginning to end, so each appointment delivers a finish you can trust.

Seven years in. Same standard. Same chair on Clarence Street. Maryam and I have trained in the Russian e-file method since 2019, and the studio is built around that craft. The work that leaves this room is meant to last four to five weeks. That's the only thing we sell.

Beyond manicure work, we offer diode laser hair removal that's safe for every skin type, Yumi Keratin lash lifts that hold for eight to twelve weeks, and brow lamination mapped to your features. Every reusable tool we use is autoclave-sterilized between clients, the same standard used for surgical instruments.

The studio is woman-owned and openly LGBTQ+ friendly. Open Wednesday through Sunday, by appointment only. We hope to see you on Clarence Street.

How we work
Page 11

Considered,
not constant.

i.

Technique-led.

Russian e-file manicure. Diode laser hair removal. Yumi keratin lash lifts. The menu is short on purpose. We do what we know we do exceptionally well.

ii.

Calm, never rushed.

Single appointments, no overlapping schedules. The room is designed to feel quiet on purpose. A drink on arrival. Time to relax.

iii.

Hygiene as baseline.

Every reusable tool autoclave-sterilized between clients. Single-use abrasives disposed of after each appointment. Hospital standard. Not a feature, a baseline.

iv.

For everyone.

Woman-owned. Openly LGBTQ+ friendly. Diode laser that works safely on every Fitzpatrick skin type. The studio is for everyone who walks through the door.
