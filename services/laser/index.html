Before your first session.

Is the laser safe for darker skin tones? +

Yes. We use a diode laser, which works safely across every skin type from Fitzpatrick I through VI. A free 15-minute consultation comes first to plan the right course for your skin and hair.

Does it hurt? +

Most clients describe the sensation as a warm pulse, not a snap. Diode laser is more comfortable than older laser technologies, even on sensitive areas.

How many sessions do I need? +

The full clinical course for permanent reduction is eight sessions. Booking the package saves you up to $393 compared to single sessions, depending on the area treated.

What's the difference between Full Face, Essentials, and Head-to-Toes? +

Full Face covers the upper lip, chin, and jawline. Essentials covers the most-treated body areas. Head-to-Toes is full-body coverage. We'll confirm the right package for you at your free consultation.
